How does it work?
Topophile helps you create 3D models of the world. Topophile teams up with 3D printing experts Shapeways.com to turn your designs into physical forms. Use Topophile to design your model and upload it to Shapeways. Order a print of your model from Shapeways and they’ll ship it to you.

Here's how you build your own model

  • 1.Select the area for your model
  • 2.Exaggerate the elevation
  • 3.Build a full model
  • 4.Choose a size
  • 5.Upload your model to Shapeways
  • 6.View your model, and order a print
  • 7.Relax
  • 8.Receive your 3D model in the mail!
  • Start Building
About Topophile

Topophile is my way of sharing my excitement for the world around me. The mountains that surround me in Seattle grab my attention when they grace the horizon. I explore them on foot and by bike, and now I explore the mountains by holding them in my hands.

The first Topophile Model Builder launches with data covering North and South America, most of Europe and a majority of Africa; it’s NASA's SRTM Global 1 dataset. Through continuing work I hope to cover the entire earth, to make more types of data accessible, and to share more models designs.
